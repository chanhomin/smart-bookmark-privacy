# Privacy Policy for Smart Bookmark

Last updated: April 7, 2026

## Overview
Smart Bookmark is a Chrome extension that captures webpage screenshots and automatically categorizes them as bookmarks using AI.

## Data We Collect
- **Page URLs and titles**: Saved locally on your device as bookmarks for personal organization.
- **Webpage screenshots**: Captured at your explicit request.

## How We Use Your Data
- Screenshots are sent to our proxy server (Cloudflare Workers) solely to request AI categorization from Google Gemini API.
- Screenshots are not stored on our servers beyond the duration of the API call.
- All bookmark data and screenshots are stored locally on your device only.

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
- Screenshots are temporarily processed by Google Gemini API for classification purposes only.
- No personally identifiable information is collected or transmitted.

## Data Storage
- All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage.
- You can delete all stored data by removing the extension.
